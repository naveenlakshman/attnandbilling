How is the Gap calculated?

This page identifies students who physically attend classes (marked Present or Late) but are lagging in their online LMS topic completion. Since each topic is designed to be completed in 1 to 1.5 hours, a high class attendance count paired with a low completed topic count indicates a performance discrepancy.
